The pipeline

Telemetry in. Radio out.

Four moving parts, one calm voice in your ear.

01

Listen

Your game streams telemetry over UDP on port 20777. airace catches every snapshot — speed, tyres, fuel, gaps, damage.

02

Route

Only the data that matters to the moment (or your question) gets sent on. Tyres query → just tyre data. Fewer tokens, faster answers.

03

Reason

A fast local model handles simple questions in seconds and only escalates the hard ones to your main model. Memory keeps it from repeating itself.

04

Speak

A Piper neural voice reads the call out loud, engineer-style. Silent until there's something worth saying.

Response time

Ask a question, get an answer in seconds. Emergencies are instant.

Your questions never wait on the slow model. Three lanes, each tuned for its job.

Everything it does

Built like a real pit wall.

A full race-engineer stack — voice, memory, routing, dashboard — running on your own hardware.

01🎙️

Voice race engineer

Speaks radio calls out loud via a Piper neural voice. Calm, terse, factual — no coaching fluff.

02🗣️

Talk back to it

Voice input via VAD + Whisper speech-to-text. Ask “gap?”, “fuel?”, “should I box?” and it answers.

03⚡

Instant event calls

Safety car, VSC, red flag, blue flag, “box this lap”, SC ending — played immediately, no AI delay.

04🧠

Smart routing

A fast 3B model handles simple questions in <3s and only escalates hard ones — with a “give us a second” bridge.

05🎯

Section routing

Sends only the telemetry that matters to your question — tyres query gets just tyre data. Fewer tokens, faster answers.

06📓

Engineer memory

Remembers recent calls and 13 topics — gap, tyres, fuel, ERS, weather, damage, pit plan, strategy, lap times — so it never repeats itself.

07📊

Live dashboard

Speed, gear, RPM, throttle/brake/steer, lap & sector times, tyre temps, and a rolling 60-second trace chart.

08📱

LAN hosting

Flip a toggle to view the dashboard from your phone or another device on your WiFi.

09🌍

9 languages

English, Español, Italiano, Français, Deutsch, Português, Čeština, Polski, 日本語.

10🔌

Bring-your-own model

OpenRouter, Groq, Anthropic, local Ollama — or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

11🎚️

Fully tunable

Chattiness slider, mute under yellow flag, suppress fuel chatter, tick interval, custom instructions, or override the whole prompt.

12🎧

Your mic & speaker

Pick any input/output device. Local or cloud (Groq) transcription. First-run wizard; desktop app or headless.

Telemetry in. Radio out.

Four moving parts, one calm voice in your ear.

01

Listen

Your game streams telemetry over UDP on port 20777. airace catches every snapshot: speed, tyres, fuel, gaps, damage.

02

Route

Only the data that matters to the moment (or your question) gets sent on. Tyres query → just tyre data. Fewer tokens, faster answers.

03

Reason

A fast model you pick handles simple questions in seconds and only escalates the hard ones to whatever heavyweight you've chosen. Memory keeps it from repeating itself.

04

Speak

A Piper neural voice reads the call out loud, engineer-style. Silent until there's something worth saying.

Any model you want. Every stage.

Nothing is hard-coded and nothing is gated. The fast lane, the heavyweight reasoner, the voice: each one is yours to pick, swap, or run fully local.

Fast lane

Quick answers

Handles “gap?”, “fuel?”, “P?” in seconds. Ships with a small local model so it's fast and free out of the box, then point it at any model you like.

Heavy reasoner

Strategy & hard calls

The model that actually thinks. Give it whatever you trust for the tricky calls, or just reuse your fast model. Your choice, no rules either way.

Voice

How it sounds

A Piper neural voice, fully local and free by default. Swap the voice, the accent, or the whole speech engine if you'd rather.

No allow-list, no “supported models” list. If it exposes an OpenAI-compatible API, airace can drive it.

Built like a real pit wall.

A full race-engineer stack: voice, memory, routing and dashboard, all running on your own hardware.

01

Voice race engineer

Speaks radio calls out loud via a Piper neural voice. Calm, terse, factual. No coaching fluff.

02

Talk back to it

Voice input via VAD + Whisper speech-to-text. Ask “gap?”, “fuel?”, “should I box?” and it answers.

03

Instant event calls

Safety car, VSC, red flag, blue flag, “box this lap”, SC ending. All played immediately, no AI delay.

04

Smart routing

A fast model of your choosing handles simple questions in <3s and only escalates hard ones to your heavyweight, with a “give us a second” bridge.

05

Section routing

Sends only the telemetry that matters to your question: a tyres query gets just tyre data. Fewer tokens, faster answers.

06

Engineer memory

Remembers recent calls and 13 topics (gap, tyres, fuel, ERS, weather, damage, pit plan, strategy, lap times) so it never repeats itself.

07

Live dashboard

Speed, gear, RPM, throttle/brake/steer, lap & sector times, tyre temps, and a rolling 60-second trace chart.

08

LAN hosting

Flip a toggle to view the dashboard from your phone or another device on your WiFi.

09

9 languages

English, Español, Italiano, Français, Deutsch, Português, Čeština, Polski, 日本語.

10

Bring-your-own model

OpenRouter, Groq, Anthropic, local Ollama, or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

11

Fully tunable

Chattiness slider, mute under yellow flag, suppress fuel chatter, tick interval, custom instructions, or override the whole prompt.

12

Your mic & speaker

Pick any input/output device. Local or cloud (Groq) transcription. First-run wizard; desktop app or headless.
